## 1. Brand Voice

### Core Voice Attributes

| Attribute | We Are | We Are Not |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Authoritative** | Confident, decisive, clear | Arrogant, jargon-heavy |
| **Warm** | Human, empathetic, direct | Cold, corporate, distant |
| **Precise** | Specific with numbers, outcomes | Vague, buzzword-driven |
| **Premium** | Elevated but not pretentious | Casual, slang-heavy |

### The Voice in Practice

```
❌ "Leverage our cutting-edge AI-powered synergies to optimize workflows."
✅ "Replace 12 tools with one. Your team moves faster. Your CFO sees why."

❌ "We are a comprehensive enterprise solution provider."
✅ "Mantle is the operating layer your enterprise stands on."

❌ "Our platform offers best-in-class features."
✅ "Reporting time dropped 60%. Managers now trust a single dashboard."
```

## 2. Headline Formulas

### Hero H1 Patterns (Bricolage Grotesque 700)

```
Pattern 1 — The Bold Statement
"[Core promise]. For the [audience]."
Example: "Run Your Enterprise on One System."

Pattern 2 — The Problem-Solution
"When [pain point], [outcome]."
Example: "When care falls through, work falls apart."

Pattern 3 — The Outcome First
"[Measurable result]. [How]."
Example: "40% less absenteeism. One platform."

Pattern 4 — The Challenge
"[Bold claim]. [Proof]."
Example: "Twelve tools. One platform. Zero exceptions."
```

### Section H2 Patterns (Bricolage Grotesque 700)

```
Pattern 1 — Action-oriented
"[Verb] your [outcome]."
Example: "Protect your people. Protect your business."

Pattern 2 — Contrast
"Not [old way]. [New way]."
Example: "Not another dashboard. The last one you'll ever need."

Pattern 3 — Question reframe
"[Expensive assumption]?"
Example: "What is tool fragmentation costing your business every year?"
```

### 3.3 Demo Flow

```
Step 1 (Qualifier):
  H2:    "Tell us about your team. We'll personalize your demo."
  Q1:    "How many employees does your company have?"
         Options: 50–200 · 201–1,000 · 1,001–5,000 · 5,000+
  Q2:    "Which Mantle modules are you most interested in?"
         Multi-select: all 8 modules
  Q3:    "What's your biggest operational challenge right now?"
         Open text — 150 char max
  Progress: "Step 1 of 3"
  After submit: "Great — we'll focus your demo on [selected modules] and show you
                 how Mantle solves [their challenge]."

Step 2 (Service Selector):
  H2:    "Which Mantle modules would you like to see in action?"
  Sub:   "Select all that apply. We'll build a custom agenda."
  Live:  "Estimated demo duration: [X] minutes" (updates as modules selected)

Step 3 (Booking Form):
  H2:    "Almost there. Book your slot."
  Fields: Full name · Work email · Job title · Phone (optional) · Preferred time
  Note:  "Timezone auto-detected. Work email auto-populates company name."

Confirmation:
  H2:    "Your demo is confirmed."
  Body:  "We'll see you on [date] at [time]. Your personalized agenda:"
         + list of selected modules
         + "Preparing anything specific for your team? Reply to your confirmation email."
  Links: Download calendar invite · Share with a colleague · Read [industry] case study
```

## 4. Microcopy Standards

### 4.1 Buttons

```
Primary actions:   "[Verb] [object] →"   — "Book a Demo →", "Start free trial →"
Destructive:       "[Verb]" plain        — "Delete project", "Revoke access"
Dismissive:        "[Noun]"              — "Cancel", "Close", "Dismiss"
Loading state:     "[Verb]ing..."        — "Booking...", "Saving..."
Success state:     "✓ [Done]"           — "✓ Demo booked", "✓ Saved"
```

### 4.2 Empty States

```
No data yet:    "[Module] is ready. Add your first [item] to get started."
No results:     "No [items] match '[query]'. Try broadening your search."
Error state:    "Something went wrong. [Link: Try again] or [Link: contact support]."
Loading:        Never show "Loading..." — use skeleton screens
```

### 4.3 Form Validation

```
Required field:   "This field is required"
Invalid email:    "Please enter a valid work email address"
Too short:        "Must be at least [N] characters"
Password weak:    "Add a number and a special character to strengthen your password"
Success:          "Looks good!" (green border only — no text needed)
```

### 4.4 Placeholder Text

```
Email inputs:     "your@company.com"
Name inputs:      "First and last name"
Search:           "Search [context]..."
Textarea:         "[One sentence describing what to enter]"
Select:           "Select [option type]"
```

## 5. Numbers & Data Formatting

```
Large numbers:     $2.4M (not $2,400,000) · 10K (not 10,000) · 1B (not 1,000,000,000)
Percentages:       40% (not 40 percent) · Always show + for positive deltas: +12%
Time:              48h (not 48 hours) · 11 min (not 11 minutes) · <3 months
Money:             $1.4M/year · $75K avg salary · Never show cents unless exact
Growth metrics:    Always show the base: "↓60% reporting time (from 8h to 3h/week)"
Ranges:            50–200 employees (en-dash, not hyphen)
Ordinals:          1st, 2nd, 3rd (not 1st., First)
```

## 6. Content Don'ts

```
❌ "Leverage"         → ✅ "use"
❌ "Utilize"          → ✅ "use"
❌ "Synergize"        → ✅ [delete entirely]
❌ "Best-in-class"    → ✅ [specific proof]
❌ "Cutting-edge"     → ✅ [specific capability]
❌ "Seamlessly"       → ✅ [specific time/step]
❌ "Comprehensive"    → ✅ [list what's included]
❌ "Robust"           → ✅ [specific metric]
❌ "World-class"      → ✅ [comparison or award]
❌ "Innovative"       → ✅ [what makes it new]
❌ "Streamline"       → ✅ [how much faster]
❌ "Empower"          → ✅ [what they can now do]
❌ "Transform"        → ✅ [before → after]
❌ "End-to-end"       → ✅ [from X to Y]
❌ "Game-changer"     → ✅ [specific outcome]
```
